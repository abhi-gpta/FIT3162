About Us

ConsumerCare was founded by a group of University students to help improve the average consumer's understanding of grocery price trends in Australia. With inflation levels and interest rates close to their all-time highs, it has never been more important for Australians to understand how these economic factors can affect their daily living costs. Using various data sources, from both the Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS) and Kaggle (independant data) we have developed a series of interactive visualisations which allow consumers to understand how products have changed in price across time and region. We also provide comparisons and analysis of price trends factoring in various economic concepts such as inflation to help users gain a more holisitic understanding of the Australian economy.
