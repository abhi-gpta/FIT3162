Average Price Trends Graph

The graph below allows users to visualise the average price trends of various product categories in comparison with each other. To use the graph below, select grocery sub-categories which you are interested in to make the comparisons visually. To make a selection, tick the checkboxes. There's a small search icon at the very top of selections to help you find the categories that you are looking for. There's a legend below the selections to tell you which coloured lines refer to which sub-categories which were ticked.
